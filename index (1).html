Why It Matters

When food enters landfills, it decomposes without oxygen, producing Methane—a greenhouse gas 25–28x more potent than CO2. This process accelerates climate change and causes:

  • Rising global temperatures (+1.1°C already recorded)
  • Soil and groundwater pollution
  • Loss of wasted energy, land, and water resources

Anaerobic Digestion (AD)

Instead of pollution, AD creates a Circular Economy. By breaking down organic waste in a controlled, oxygen-free environment, we capture methane rather than releasing it.


The Result: Renewable Energy + Digestate.

The Power of Digestate

Digestate is the nutrient-rich material left after digestion, containing Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium. It serves as:

  • Natural, organic fertilizer
  • Soil enhancer and compost
  • Animal bedding and bio-product raw material

Sustainability Goals

AD reduces landfill dependency, improves soil health, and creates green jobs. It transforms a "waste problem" into a "resource solution."
