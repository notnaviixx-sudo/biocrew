Executive Summary

Turning Waste into Value

Food loss and food waste are both major global crises with roughly one third of all food produced ending up wasted, that is approximately 1.3 billion tons. This represents a waste of water, land, energy, and labor used in production. Our project introduces a sustainable alternative through anaerobic digestion technology, converting food scraps into biogas for fuel and nutrient rich fertilizers.

National Statistics

Saudi Arabia Waste Analysis

In Saudi Arabia, food waste costs the economy around SAR 40 billion annually. Approximately 33% of food is wasted, totaling 4 million tons per year. Our solution targets these specific areas of loss to maximize recovery.

Environmental Wellness

Impact & Sustainability

Converting food waste into biogas or fertilizer transforms waste into useful resources. This approach reduces landfill waste, lowers environmental impacts, and supports a more circular and sustainable food system.
